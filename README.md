# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore Sales dataset. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping modes, payment methods, and product categories.

The goal of the project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights through data visualization and KPI tracking.

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales, quantity, and profit performance.
- Compare monthly sales and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing customer segments.
- Evaluate shipping mode preferences.
- Analyze product category and sub-category performance.
- Understand payment method distribution.
- Compare Year-over-Year (YoY) sales and profit growth.

## Key Insights

- Consumer segment contributes approximately 50% of total sales.
- Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode.
- Office Supplies generate the highest sales among all categories.
- Phones are the top-performing sub-category.
- COD is the most used payment method.
- Strong sales growth is observed during the final quarter of the year.
